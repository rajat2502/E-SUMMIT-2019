THE ENTREPRENEURSHIP SUMMIT NOIDA 2019

The True entrepreneur is a doer, not a dreamer. There are numerous number of start-ups that born everyday, but only a few survive , And everyone desires to be among the few. There comes the realization to do. This is what we aim to do in the upcoming E-Summit. Learn about the dynamics of business, the bases to grow on and much more. Lay the foundation of your future today. This E-Summit surely is not to be missed. On the 20th and 21st April, 2019, EDC, JSSATE is all set to conduct its flagship event, the E-Summit , Noida. With a promise to usher the entrepreneurial skills of the students, the E-Summit’19 has much more than its former versions. This E-Summit will be a convocation of eminent speakers from the business industry, workshop on skill development, B-plan competitions and an internship fair for the skillful students. So, don’t wait! Get your entrepreneurial feet wet and move a step ahead to make your dream a reality.
